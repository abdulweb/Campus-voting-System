Welcome to RODAB College

RODAB COLLEGE is a new world-class Muslim secondary school borne out of a concerted effort to provide an enabling environment for the Muslim child to excel despite the challenges by the complex but delicate interaction betwen expectations of the parents, religious values, culture and the child's God's-given potential & talents in an emerging ICT driven, morally downgraded society.

Our Vision

To provide an all-encompassing, integrated system of education that will produce academically sound, morally upright and proficiently skilled Muslim children that can compete well with contemporaries world over while maintaining high standard of Islamic ethics that guarantees success bith in this world and the hereafter.

Our Mission

To create an academic environment embellished with standard educational equipment and seasoned teachers endowed with modern teaching styles and teaching aids that will spontaneoulsy stimulate the interest to learn and instinctively unleash the God's-given potentials and capabilities of the children which will be harnessed to achieve supreme success.
